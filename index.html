Our History

What started as a small independent restaurant, has transformed into a popular family business. Nico’s was founded by Nicolas Moran Carreon in San Diego, and in 1993, his brother Prieto opened the first Nico’s in Arizona. Today, 23 locations can be found scattered across San Diego and Arizona. In 2023, the very first Nico’s international location opened its doors in Eindhoven. All locations are run by the children and grandchildren of Nicolas and his brothers and sisters. And business continues to grow; but make no mistake, Nico’s is not a chain or franchise (at least for now).
